## Author flow recommendation

Don't author top-to-bottom. Better order:

1. **Anchor concepts first** (Q16-27) — establish your hockey-sense voice. These are the brand-defining questions; everything else echoes their tone.
2. **Skills next** (Q1-15) — concrete, easy to write, lots of pov-mc reps.
3. **Compete + goalie** (Q37-48) — habits, clear rules, plenty of `tf` opportunities.
4. **Tactics last** (Q28-36) — the softened-at-U7 set. By this point you know the voice.

Within each section, BATCH BY VISUAL CONTEXT:
- Compose ONE Scene Composer scene
- "+ New Question" multiple times against it (same image, multiple concepts)
- Each pov-mc gets the same image but a different stem + answer
- This is the multi-question-per-scene pattern from your Notion workflow.

A single rink-front-of-net scene can spawn questions on: dz-coverage, decision-making, reading-the-play, body-position. Use the same image; vary the stem.
